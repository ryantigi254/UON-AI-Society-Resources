**Post 3: Case Studies: Successful Classical ML Models**
1. **Email Filtering:** Machine learning models that can distinguish between spam and important emails save us time every day.
2. **Credit Scoring:** Banks use machine learning to assess creditworthiness, making lending decisions faster and more accurately.
3. **Health Diagnostics:** ML algorithms help in diagnosing diseases by analyzing medical images and patient data.
4. **Customer Segmentation:** Businesses use clustering algorithms to segment customers for targeted marketing campaigns.
5. **Predictive Maintenance:** Machine learning predicts equipment failures before they happen, avoiding costly downtimes.
