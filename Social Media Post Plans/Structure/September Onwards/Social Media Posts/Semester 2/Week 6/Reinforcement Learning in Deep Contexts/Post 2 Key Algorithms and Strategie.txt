**Post 2: Key Algorithms and Strategies in DRL**
1. **Exploring DQN:** Dive deeper into the Deep Q-Network algorithm, implementing it in a simulated environment to understand its learning process.
2. **Policy Gradient Methods:** Experiment with policy gradient methods, focusing on how they optimize the policy directly for long-term rewards.
3. **Actor-Critic Methods:** Understand actor-critic methods by building a model that combines value-based and policy-based approaches for more stable learning.
4. **Advanced DRL Techniques:** Explore advanced techniques in DRL, such as trust region methods and distributional DRL, to enhance agent performance and robustness.
5. **Hyperparameter Tuning in DRL:** Learn about the importance of hyperparameter tuning in DRL, employing techniques like grid search or Bayesian optimization to find optimal settings.
