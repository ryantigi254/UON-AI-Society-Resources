**Post 2: Synthetic Data Generation for Training Models**
1. **The Role of Synthetic Data:** Understand how synthetic data can supplement real datasets, particularly in scenarios where data collection is challenging or privacy concerns exist.
2. **Generating Synthetic Data:** Use generative models to produce synthetic datasets, ensuring they're diverse and representative enough for effective model training.
3. **Privacy-Preserving Data:** Explore techniques for generating privacy-preserving synthetic data that maintains the utility of real datasets without exposing sensitive information.
4. **Synthetic Data in Simulations:** Apply synthetic data in simulations or virtual environments to train models for tasks like autonomous driving or robotics.
5. **Quality Assessment:** Learn methods to assess the quality and usability of synthetic data, ensuring it contributes positively to model performance.
