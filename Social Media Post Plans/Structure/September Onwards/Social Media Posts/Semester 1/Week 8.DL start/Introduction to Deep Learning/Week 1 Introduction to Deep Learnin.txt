**Week 1: Introduction to Deep Learning**

**Post 1: Demystifying Deep Learning**
1. **Explore the Origins:** Begin by understanding deep learning's roots in artificial intelligence and neural networks, appreciating its capacity to mimic human-like learning.
2. **Visualize Neural Networks:** Use online resources or software to visualize how neural networks process information, laying the groundwork for deeper learning.
3. **Identify Deep Learning Impacts:** Research and list ways deep learning is transforming industries like healthcare, finance, and entertainment, recognizing its widespread influence.
4. **Deep vs. Traditional Learning:** Compare deep learning with traditional machine learning through examples, noting the complexity and types of problems each can solve.
5. **Discover Deep Learning Applications:** Investigate real-world applications of deep learning, noting how it powers technologies like voice assistants and recommendation systems.
