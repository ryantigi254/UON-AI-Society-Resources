**Post 3: The Future of Ethical ML**
1. **Regulatory Compliance:** ML systems must comply with emerging regulations that govern data usage, privacy, and ethical standards.
2. **Inclusive Design:** The design and development of ML models should be inclusive, considering the broad spectrum of users and scenarios.
3. **Societal Impact:** Understanding the societal impact of ML applications is crucial for developing technology that benefits humanity.
4. **Human-in-the-loop:** Keeping human oversight in ML processes ensures that ethical considerations are weighed in decision-making.
5. **Future Frameworks:** Developing ethical frameworks and guidelines for ML will be pivotal in guiding the responsible development and deployment of AI technologies.
